The USA composes about 38.54% of the total recorded shark attacks between 1898-2022 with a total of 2336 incidents.

Australia composes about 21.4% of total shark attacks with a total of 1299 incidents.

South Africa composes about 9.3% of total shark attacks with a total of 564 incidents.

Other countries that have exceeded 100 recorded attacks from 1898-2022 include BRAZIL and NEW ZEALAND.

MEXICO, ITALY, NEW CALEDONIA, PHILIPPHINES, REUNION, and FIJI have over 50, but less than 100 shark attacks between 1898-2022.

The USA, Australia, and South Africa combined account for over half of history's recorded shark attacks and there is reason to belive that there is more at play contributing the shark attack incidents than exponential population growth.
